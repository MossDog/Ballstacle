# Ballstacle

Ballstacle is a physics-based platformer and passion project, designed for release on Steam. The game will offer two distinct modes of play:

- **Story Mode**: A chapter and level-based experience with smaller, themed levels.
- **Foddian Mode**: A single, long, and challenging level designed to test your skills and patience.

## Target Audience

Ballstacle is designed for players who enjoy games like *Trials* and *Trackmania*. It caters to both casual and competitive players, with a competitive aspect where players can compete to achieve the best times on various levels.

## Development Details

- **Engine**: Godot 4.5.
